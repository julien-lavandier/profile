003 : Metrics for air traffic complexity evaluation

Developement of metrics to evaluate complexity of air traffic situations, based on the dynamic of the situations.

Complexity metrics are used to evaluate controllers' workload for different air traffic situations. The capacity of a sector can be determined by the number of aircraft a controller can manage. However, this number can change in function of the geometry of the situation. For example, parallel aircraft does not require a lot of process from the controller.
